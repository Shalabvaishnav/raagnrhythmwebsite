About Us

Building Musicians,
Not Just Students

At Raag & Rhythm Academy, our vision is to make high-quality music education accessible across India, nurturing not just skilled musicians but individuals with a deep and lifelong connection to music.

Whether a student aspires to perform professionally or simply pursue music as a passion, our goal is to provide the knowledge, discipline, and real-world musical exposure needed to truly grow.
